Color Theory
  • Color Palette

Red

Red color symbolizes powerful & intense emotions, ranging from passion, and desire to anger, war, & danger

White

White color symbolizes purity, innocence, & represents new beginnings, fresh start, & spirituality.

Pink

Pink color symbolizes warmth, and compassion, with connotations of feminity, youth, & gentleness.

Purple

Purple color symbolizes royalty, luxury, power, wisdom, & mystery.

Blue

Blue color symbolizes trust, calm, & authority in Western cultures, representing stability & serenity.

Black

Black color symblizes a wide range of concepts, including power, elegance, and sophisticated, but also mourning, death, & evil in Western culture.

Green

Green primary symbolizes nature, growth, freshness, harmony, and renewal due to its connection with plant life.

Yellow

Yellow primarily symbolizes happiness, warmth, & sunshine, but it is also associated with caution, intellect, & creativity due to its high visibility & stimulating properties.

Orange

Orange color signifies a vibrant mix of warmth, enthusiasm, & creativity, stemming from its balance blend of red & yellow.
